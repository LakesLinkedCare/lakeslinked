About the site

Lakes Linked is an independent public-health analytics project operated by Sergey Soshnikov, MD PhD. The disease burden analyses were designed and implemented independently, with all calculations reproduced in Python from primary data sources.

Data and estimates

All estimates — DALYs, mortality rates, prevalence, and related indicators — are planning-grade approximations, not formal GBD study outputs. They are derived from publicly available sources including CDC WONDER, U.S. Census Bureau, and IHME GBD study data. Estimates carry uncertainty; values should be treated as directional rather than definitive.

You may use county estimates for:

  • Public health planning and needs assessment
  • Grant applications and funding proposals
  • Academic research and teaching (with attribution)
  • Journalism and policy advocacy

You may not use estimates to make individual clinical decisions, diagnoses, or treatment recommendations.
