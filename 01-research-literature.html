02 On the preprint servers

Where the field is moving before peer review catches up — medRxiv, bioRxiv, arXiv.

medRxiv · year in review

AI in Healthcare: 2025 Year in Review

The field is maturing from exploratory development toward real-world evaluation and deployment. The review spans 2,966 publications in 2025 — up from 1,946 in 2024. Imaging stays the dominant data type (53.9%), then text (38.2%), with audio climbing to 1.2% as multimodal models spread. Leading specialties: imaging, head & neck, surgery, oncology, ophthalmology.
